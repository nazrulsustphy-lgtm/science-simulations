Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day free trial work?
Sign up and start your Premium trial with full access to everything. No credit card required. After 7 days, you can choose to continue with Premium or go back to Free — whichever suits you.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel your Premium subscription anytime from your dashboard. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
What happens if I cancel Premium?
Your account switches to Free plan after the billing period ends. You keep all your saved data, favorites, and quiz history. Unused monthly requests expire, but credit pack requests never expire.
How long does a custom simulation take?
Typically same day. Queue position depends on current demand. You'll be notified by email and website notification when ready.
Are simulations scientifically accurate?
Yes. Every simulation goes through a 6-pillar quality check including scientific accuracy (minimum 8/10 score). Users can also report any errors they find.
Do you offer refunds?
Since we offer a 7-day free trial, all paid subscriptions are non-refundable. However, you can cancel anytime and your access continues until the end of the billing period.
