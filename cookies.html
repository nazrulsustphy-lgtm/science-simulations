Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This policy explains what cookies and similar technologies we use, and why.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. "localStorage" is similar — data kept in your browser. We use both for functionality and user experience.

Cookies We Use

TypePurposeDuration
EssentialAuthentication, session managementSession / 30 days
FunctionalUser preferences, dark mode, favorites1 year

Rate LimitingPrevent spam (comment limits)1 hour

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication cookies
  • Google OAuth — Sign-in cookies (if you use Google login)
  • Lemon Squeezy — Payment cookies (only during checkout)

Your Choices

You can control cookies through:

  • Cookie banner — Accept or reject non-essential cookies
  • Browser settings — Block or delete cookies anytime
  • Incognito mode — Cookies cleared when you close the window

Note: Rejecting essential cookies will prevent you from signing in and using the service.
