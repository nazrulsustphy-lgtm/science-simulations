Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

At SciSim ("we", "our", "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our interactive science simulation platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture (if available). We use this to create and manage your account.

Usage Data

We track which simulations you view, quiz results, comments you post, and custom requests you submit. This helps improve the service and show you relevant content.

Technical Data

We collect country-level location (not precise IP) for analytics. We use cookies and localStorage for authentication and session management.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve the SciSim service
  • Personalize content recommendations
  • Process payments (via Lemon Squeezy)
  • Send essential notifications (simulation ready, comment replies)
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — Our database and authentication provider
  • Google — For authentication (OAuth)
  • Lemon Squeezy — Payment processing (only for Premium users)
  • Legal authorities — If required by law

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, UK, or similar jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access — Download all your data from Dashboard → Settings
  • Rectification — Update your profile information
  • Erasure — Delete your account permanently (Dashboard → Settings)
  • Portability — Export your data as JSON
  • Object — Contact us to opt out of specific processing
  • Withdraw consent — Sign out and delete your account

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and localStorage for caching. Non-essential cookies (analytics) require your consent via the cookie banner.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. Upon deletion, all data is permanently removed within 30 days (backups may persist up to 90 days).

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

SciSim is intended for users 13 years and older. If you are under 13, please do not use this service. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, Row Level Security (RLS) on our database, and secure authentication via Google OAuth.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or website banner.

10. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@scisim.app

This policy is written in plain English for clarity. If translated, the English version takes precedence in legal disputes.
